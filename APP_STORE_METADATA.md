## Description
Evrywher breaks every language barrier. Chat with anyone in any language — AI handles the translation in real-time. Scan documents, translate signs with your camera, learn vocabulary from your real conversations, and navigate life in Japan with cultural guidance.

Features:
- Real-time chat translation (20+ languages)
- Camera translation — point at signs, menus, forms
- Voice translation — speak and hear in any language
- Document scanner with AI text extraction
- Cultural Coach — say it right in Japanese
- Emergency Card — offline safety phrases
- Business Card Scanner — scan and save contacts
- Language Learning — learn from your real life, not a textbook
- Form Fill Guide — AI helps you fill Japanese forms
- Beautiful dark mode interface

Free to start. Pro unlocks unlimited translations, voice features, and all learning content.
